# Computational Enrichment Analysis of SAP Protein on Lysosomal Storage Disorder (MLD)

**Type:** Undergraduate Thesis Research  
**Disease Focus:** Metachromatic Leukodystrophy (MLD) — a rare Lysosomal Storage Disorder  
**Tools:** Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, ML enrichment analysis

## Overview
Original undergraduate research investigating the computational enrichment 
analysis of Saposin (SAP) protein and its role in Metachromatic Leukodystrophy, 
a rare and fatal neurological disorder caused by enzyme deficiency in the 
lysosomal pathway.

- Full thesis paper with computational methodology and findings
- Conference/symposium poster presentation
